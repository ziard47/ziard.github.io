About Us

 

Frilux (Sun Shades) is a medium sized business, which specializes in selling imported sunglass items such as Burberry, Armani Exchange, Oakley, Ray-Ban and many more.

Frilux (Sun Shades) has more than 5 branches Island wide, and expecting to expand in the near future to other cities as well, since they sell high quality goods and they offer high level of good customer service, has won a good place in the market for their business.

Our Vision

 

Our vision it to secure undisputed global category leadership as the destination of choice for eyecare solutions, leveraging our best-in-class customer value proposition.

Our Mission

 

Our mission is to provide the overall best service for people's vision needs. Our strategy is to offer people a vast selection of the highest quality frames and lenses at great prices. We support these offerings with access to information and friendly attention. We help people to define their personal beauty, and to see the beauty in the world.
